**Triage rationale:**

`NEEDS_LIVE_CONTEXT` is the correct verdict here, not `NEEDS_LOCAL_PROOF` or `HIGH_CONFIDENCE_CANDIDATE`, for the following reasons:

1. **No source, no selectors.** `unknown_verification_status` means the pipeline could not confirm whether the contract is verified on BscScan. No ABI exists in the brief. Without selectors, no call sequence can be constructed.

2. **No deployer, no cluster.** Both `deployer_address` and `funding_cluster_id` are empty strings. The `recon_bravo_then_corecritical` routing in `sentinel/scoring.py` fires as the **else-fallthrough** — it means none of the more specific routing conditions (cluster risk, unverified contract, price spike, bot, proxy change, reward pool, bridge, vault, lending) matched. [1](#0-0) 

3. **Signal is real but opaque.** The `balance_spike`, `immediate_balance_spike`, `hidden_high_value_contract`, and `audit_hidden_contract` tags are high-signal for a custody surface worth investigating. The $737K TVL is above the BSC `target_min_value_usd` threshold of $200K. [2](#0-1)  The value is real; the interface is unknown.

4. **Hard rule: prefer REJECT over speculation.** No exploit family can be confirmed without selectors. The path hypothesis is entirely hypothetical. Escalating to `NEEDS_LOCAL_PROOF` would require at minimum a known selector and a plausible access-control gap. [3](#0-2)
